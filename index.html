Craftsmanship

The essence of VELTRIN begins long before a watch ever reaches the wrist.

In our ateliers, every movement and component is conceived with purpose and scrutinized at every stage of creation. We design not for momentary impression, but for enduring reliability; not for spectacle, but for mechanical truth.

Precision is not assumed - it is verified. Each gear, each surface, each tolerance is measured and examined, guided by standards as exacting as those found in the most established manufactories. Every detail is tested not just for performance in isolation, but for harmony within the whole.

Here, craftsmanship is not a claim - it is a methodical discipline, rooted in meticulous work, deep expertise, and thoughtful restraint.

Frequently Asked

What materials are used in VELTRIN watches?

Each model uses carefully selected materials: 316L stainless steel cases, sapphire crystal, premium leather or metal bands. Full specifications available on each product page.

How accurate are VELTRIN watches?

Our automatic movements maintain ±5 seconds per month accuracy, verified through our rigorous testing protocols.

What is the warranty?

All VELTRIN watches come with a 10-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Movement servicing available every 5 years.

How long is the wait time for delivery?

Standard orders ship within 2-4 weeks. Made-to-order customizations: 6-8 weeks.
